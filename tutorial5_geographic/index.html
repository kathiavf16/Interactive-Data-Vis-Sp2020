Location of Petroleum Fields In The U.S.A.

The petroleum dataset (PETRODATA) is a dataset of oil and gas fields throughout the world. It includes information about the geographic location of hydrocarbon reserves and is specifically designed for display, manipulation and analysis in geographic information systems (GIS).
I sorted my data to only show the locations of petroleum fields in the USA.

Source: https://www.prio.org/Data/Geographical-and-Resource-Datasets/Petroleum-Dataset/Petroleum-Dataset-v-12/
